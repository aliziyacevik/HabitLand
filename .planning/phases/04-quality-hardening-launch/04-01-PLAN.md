       - **HealthKitManager.swift** (2 prints, category: healthkit):

         - CloudKit fallback print -> `HLLogger.data.error("CloudKit ModelContainer failed, falling back to local-only: \(error.localizedDescription, privacy: .public)")`

       - **HabitLandApp.swift** (2 prints, category: app):
