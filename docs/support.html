Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Profile > Settings > tap "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen. Your Pro status will be restored if you've previously purchased.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > HabitLand Pro > Cancel. Your Pro features remain active until the current billing period ends.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile > Settings > Data & Export > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your habits, sleep logs, achievements, and profile data.

How does the free plan work?

The free plan lets you track up to 3 habits with full streak tracking, weekly overview, and reminders. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited habits, sleep tracking, social features, and more.
